About the Diner

The Rock & Roll Diner brings the 50’s back to the 21st century. With a variety selection of foods to choose from, you can enjoy a nice dinner with friends and family. Therefore, come enjoy some well-cooked burgers and seasoned fries all while having groovy music playing throughout the diner. So, bring your best puffy skirt and leather pants to Rock & Roll Diner and get into the 50’s vibes.
