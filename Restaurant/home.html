About the Diner

Looking for a great view of the city while eating tasty food? Then come to The Skyline Diner! Experience a relaxing evening with your friends and family and make memories you’ll keep forever. The Skyline Diner depends on the darkness of the night in order to offer their customers the best view of the city, therefore, the diner opens at 5pm as the sun begins to set.

Our founder had an idea to create a restaurant that was more than a place that served great food. The Skyline Diner gives the locals a place where they'd always feel magical. A place where they'd be treated right by people who have a passion to serve. We treat people right, and that's what makes new guests feel welcome, and regulars feel right at home.

We're loved for our juicy burgers, our tender fries, our specialty drinks, and more. Our inviting, comfortable dining room is the perfect place to enjoy a great dinner....and don’t forget to stay a little later for our famous Salsa Nights every Thursday. You’ll experience nothing less than energetic and lively atmosphere here at The Skyline Diner.
